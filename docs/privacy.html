Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 14, 2026

Overview

SkyBrief is a preflight weather briefing app for general aviation pilots. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data SkyBrief collects, how it is used, and your rights.

Authentication

SkyBrief uses Firebase Authentication to manage sign-in. You can sign in with Google or with an email and password. We store the following account information:

  • Email address
  • Display name
  • Firebase user ID (anonymous identifier)

This data is stored securely by Google Firebase and is used solely to identify your account within the app.

Location Data

If you grant location permission, SkyBrief uses your coordinates to find nearby weather stations. Location data is processed entirely on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Weather Data

SkyBrief fetches weather information (METARs, TAFs, NOTAMs, and charts) directly from aviationweather.gov, a public API operated by NOAA and the National Weather Service. No personal data is sent with these requests.

Local Storage

Your preferences, personal minimums, saved aircraft profiles, and other settings are stored locally on your device using MMKV (a high-performance key-value store). This data never leaves your device.

Analytics & Tracking

SkyBrief does not use any analytics services, advertising SDKs, or third-party tracking tools. We do not collect usage data, crash reports, or behavioral information.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase Authentication (Google) — for user sign-in only
  • aviationweather.gov (NOAA/NWS) — public weather data

No other third-party services receive data from SkyBrief.

Data Retention

Your Firebase account data is retained as long as your account exists. You can delete your account at any time from the Settings screen in the app. Local data is removed when you uninstall the app.

Children's Privacy

SkyBrief is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at skybrief.app@gmail.com.
